Account Deletion

How to Delete Your HisoHiso Account and Data

Self-Service Account Deletion

HisoHiso provides a simple and straightforward way for users to delete their account and associated data directly from within the app. You have full control over your account and can initiate the deletion process at any time without needing to contact support.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the HisoHiso app on your mobile device
  2. Navigate to your Profile by tapping the profile icon in the navigation bar
  3. Access Account Settings by tapping the settings gear icon
  4. Select "Account Information" from the settings menu
  5. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account" (displayed in red)
  6. Confirm your decision by following the on-screen prompts
  7. Your account will be permanently deleted after confirmation

Important: Before You Delete Your Account

  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible
  • All your posts, comments, and interactions will be permanently removed
  • Your username will become available for others to use
  • You will lose access to all your saved content and favorites
  • Any active subscriptions or premium features will be terminated

What Data Is Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (username, bio, profile picture)
  • All posts and content you've created
  • Comments and interactions on other users' posts
  • Your followers and following lists
  • Saved posts and bookmarks
  • Location history and preferences
  • App settings and customizations
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Data Retention Policy

Upon account deletion, your personal data will be immediately marked for deletion and removed from our active databases. However, please note:

  • Some data may remain in backup systems for up to 30 days
  • Anonymized or aggregated data that doesn't identify you may be retained
  • Data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes

Alternative: Temporary Deactivation

If you're not ready to permanently delete your account, consider temporarily deactivating it instead. This option allows you to take a break from HisoHiso while preserving your data for potential future use.

Need Assistance?

While account deletion is designed to be a self-service process through the app, we understand that you may occasionally need help. If you encounter any issues with the deletion process or have questions about your data, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Account Recovery

Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered. If you wish to use HisoHiso again in the future, you will need to create a new account. Your previous username may not be available if it has been taken by another user.
