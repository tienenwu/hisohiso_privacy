How do I post anonymously?
When creating a post, simply toggle the "Post Anonymously" switch. Your identity will be protected and other users won't be able to see your personal information.
How does location privacy work?
HisoHiso uses fuzzy location technology. Your exact location is never displayed. All location information is randomly offset within a certain range to ensure your privacy and safety.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Long press any post or comment and select the "Report" option. Our team will review and take action within 24 hours.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account Management > Delete Account. Please note that this action cannot be undone and all your data will be permanently deleted.

For detailed instructions, please visit: Account Deletion Guide
What languages are supported?
Currently we support Traditional Chinese, Simplified Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Russian, and Vietnamese.
