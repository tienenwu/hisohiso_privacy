HisoHiso Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Last Updated: January 11, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: This Privacy Policy explains how HisoHiso Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with global privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, and APAC privacy regulations.

🔒 WE DO NOT SELL YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

Our Commitment to Your Privacy:

  • We NEVER sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties
  • We NEVER share your data for commercial purposes or monetary gain
  • We ONLY share data with essential service providers needed to operate our app
  • Your data is NEVER sold to advertisers, data brokers, or marketing companies
  • Our business model is based on providing quality service, not selling user data

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, profile picture, and authentication credentials
  • Content: Posts, comments, messages, photos, and videos you share
  • Profile Data: Bio, interests, and preferences you add to your profile

1.2 Location Information

As a location-based social app, we collect:

  • Precise Location: GPS coordinates when you create posts or use location features
  • Approximate Location: City and country from your IP address
  • Location History: Places you've visited to provide personalized recommendations
  • Fuzzy Location: We automatically blur exact locations to protect your privacy

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique identifiers
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app, features used, time spent
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, network information
  • Analytics Data: Performance metrics and user behavior (only with consent)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide Our Services

  • Display location-based content and posts near you
  • Enable social features like comments, likes, and shares
  • Connect you with other users in your area
  • Provide personalized content recommendations

2.2 To Improve Our Services

  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Optimize app performance and speed

2.3 For Safety and Security

  • Detect and prevent fraud, spam, and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  • Protect users from harmful content
  • Respond to legal requests and comply with laws

3. Information Sharing

3.1 We Share Information With:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (data storage), Google Maps (location services), and other essential services
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety Protection: To protect rights, property, or safety of our users
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition (with same privacy protections)

3.2 We NEVER Share With:

  • Advertisers or ad networks
  • Data brokers or information resellers
  • Marketing companies
  • Any third party for monetary gain

4. Your Privacy Rights

🇪🇺 GDPR Rights (European Union)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Delete your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Object: Opt-out of certain data processing
  • Restrict: Limit how we use your data

🇺🇸 CCPA Rights (California)

  • Know: What personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: We don't sell data, but you can opt-out of analytics
  • Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

🇧🇷 LGPD Rights (Brazil)

  • Confirmation: Confirm if we process your data
  • Access: Access your personal data
  • Correction: Correct incomplete or inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Delete unnecessary data
  • Portability: Transfer data to another service

🌏 APAC Rights (Asia-Pacific)

  • Japan (APPI): Full transparency and consent-based data handling
  • South Korea (PIPA): Clear data processing with user control
  • Singapore (PDPA): Consent withdrawal and data correction rights
  • Taiwan (PDPA): Data access, correction, and deletion rights

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access with role-based permissions
  • Security Monitoring: 24/7 monitoring for suspicious activities
  • Regular Audits: Quarterly security assessments
  • Secure Infrastructure: Firebase's enterprise-grade security

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Posts & Content: Kept until you delete them
  • Location Data: You can delete anytime from settings
  • Deleted Data: Removed from active systems within 30 days
  • Backups: Purged from backups within 90 days

7. Analytics and Cookies

Google Analytics (Non-EU Regions):

  • Analytics is enabled by default to improve app performance
  • Only anonymized, aggregated data is collected
  • No personally identifiable information is shared
  • You can disable analytics anytime in Settings → Privacy

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

As a global service, your data may be transferred to and processed in different countries. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable privacy laws and maintain the same level of protection described in this policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email notifications (if you've provided an email)
  • Prominent notice in the app

11. How to Exercise Your Rights

You can exercise your privacy rights through:

  • In-App: Settings → Account → Export/Delete Data
  • Email: Contact us at service@hisohiso.app
  • Response Time: Within 30 days (or as required by law)
